Our Objectives

Essa is a career oriented club

Foster Economic Reasoning

Essa club disseminates Economic knowledge and the results of economic reserch through the exchange of ideas and the application of economic knowledge in our current economy.

Organize Workshops

Our club organize Workshops to ensure that we provide an excelent forum for specialized presentaions. The club stimulate dicussions and intrests in current reserch and issues in our economic world.

Publicize Career Opportunities

The club supports educational training to all students in the economic industry. We provide adequate professional support to all our members. Essa is a gateway to a bright career path.

Our Activities

Join us to enjoy our services

Career Talk

Essa Club in conjuction with the University offers career advice to all students. The aim of the career talk is to guide youngstars in choosing the best career paths.

Data Analysis

Statistics being part of our study, it is of pivotal importance to equip our members with statistical know-how in data analysis . Our club organizes data analysis programes per semister.

Natonal Symposium Debates

Essa club members participate in both local and national debates. Through the active involvement in matters pertaining Economics we are fully ready to fill up the economic cracks.
